## What this tests

After per-family intercept calibration, the family-level totals are exact by construction.
This diagnostic checks whether within-family residual bias remains across the AADT
distribution. Persistent within-family AADT bias would indicate that the residual problem
is a slope (or feature) issue, not an intercept issue — and that per-family GLMs with
separate exposure coefficients are needed.

AADT deciles are computed separately within each family (decile 0 = lowest 10% of AADT
*within that family*, not across the full population).
